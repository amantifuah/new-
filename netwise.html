Digital Safety For
Young People

What is
digital safety?

Digital safety (often called online or internet safety) is the knowledge, behavior, and practices that help protect individuals, especially young people, from risks and dangers while using digital technologies like the internet, social media, mobile apps, and connected devices. â€‹It is about being a careful and responsible user and citizen of the digital world.

Why is digital safety important?

Digital safety is crucial because it protects our personal information, finances, and well-being from cyber threats like hackers, scams, malware, and identity theft; safeguards against online harms like bullying, grooming, and inappropriate content; and builds essential skills for responsible online behavior, ensuring we can use the internet's benefits without undue risk to our mental health or physical security.
